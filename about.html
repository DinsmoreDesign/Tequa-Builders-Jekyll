The Phoenix-Metro contractors you can trust.

Owned by the former president of Paniolo Builders, Tequa Builders, LLC is a residential and commercial general contractor that is licensed, bonded and insured and in excellent standing with the Arizona Registrar of Contractors. With over 30 years of experience in the construction industry, you’ll see why we’ve quickly become one of Arizona’s premier builders.

Learn about our team...

J.D. Dinsmore

JD is a 40-year resident of Arizona and has over 30 years of experience as a General Contractor, Project and Construction Manager in the home building / development and solar industries. His background includes research, evaluation, planning and implementation of construction projects in diverse environments within metropolitan Phoenix; JD is also a candidate for LEED Green & LEED AP certification.

Besides being in the home building / development industries, JD was the Project Manager for one of the largest thermal solar projects (Phase-1) on a resort in the state of AZ at the time, at the Hyatt Regency Resort in Scottsdale, AZ. He is an advocate of renewable energy and member of the HBACA and ASBA.

Philip Harel

Phil is a 20-year resident of Arizona with over 30 years of experience as a Contractor, Construction Manager / Project Superintendent in the home building and remodeling industries. His background includes the building of custom and production homes in the states of Connecticut and Arizona.

Phil is an accomplished craftsman and carpenter, able to create anything that you can envision. The quality of his work is unequaled in the building industry, with his excellent attention to detail.

Brian C. Baker

Brian has been a resident of Fountain Hills, Arizona for many years. Brian has over 30 years of experience in the building and solar industries. He is a licensed General Contractor in Arizona, Connecticut, Massachusetts and North Carolina.

Brian is a longtime advocate of renewable energy and is experienced in efficient and sustainable building materials and techniques. He is a candidate for LEED AP certification.

In addition to being part of the Tequa team, Brian also owns and operates Southwest Commercial Solar. He holds bachelor’s degrees in mathematics and physics from CCSU, and a master’s degree in finance from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. He is a member of SEIA.
